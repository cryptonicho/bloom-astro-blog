## What is Self-Care?

Self-care encompasses any activity that we do deliberately to take care of our mental, emotional, and physical health.

## Quick Self-Care Ideas

- **Morning meditation** - Just 5 minutes can set the tone for your day
- **Gratitude journaling** - Write down 3 things you're grateful for
- **Nature walks** - Connect with nature during your lunch break
- **Digital detox** - Set boundaries with technology

## Building a Sustainable Routine

The key to effective self-care is consistency, not perfection. Start small and build habits that work for your lifestyle.

Remember: You can't pour from an empty cup. Taking care of yourself enables you to better care for others.
